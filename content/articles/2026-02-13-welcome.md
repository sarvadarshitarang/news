Welcome to **SarvadarshiTarang**, your modern news platform that runs entirely for free!

## What Makes This Special?

This is not just another news app. It's a complete serverless architecture that costs you **absolutely nothing** to run:

- ✅ **GitHub** - Free hosting for content and code
- ✅ **GitHub Pages** - Free CDN for your app and JSON feed
- ✅ **GitHub Actions** - Free automation (2000 minutes/month)
- ✅ **Flutter** - Build once, deploy everywhere

## How It Works

1. **Write** articles in Markdown format
2. **Commit** to your GitHub repository
3. **Automated** conversion to JSON feed
4. **Instant** updates in your app

No servers, no databases, no monthly bills!

## Getting Started

Check out the documentation in the repository to start publishing your first article. It's as simple as creating a markdown file!
